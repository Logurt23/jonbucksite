What is Gift Card of Time?

G.C.O.T. allows you to give gift cards of your time to loved ones, friends, or someone special.
-- Fully customizable cards, time redemptions, and descriptions. GCOT is shipped right to you or your desired addressee -- We have Standard and Premium cards avaliable.

  • Date November 20, 2020
  • Location Joplin, Missouri

How it Works


Gift Card of Time is a simple and fun product that allows you to gift your time in the form of a custom gift-card. This is a -Physical Card- that is shipped directly to either your or the recipient's address after purchase. You can customize the card from color, material, how much time it contains, and more.

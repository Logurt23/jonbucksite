Sponsor A Veteran

Our Goal For Sponsor A Veteran

Our goal is to provide funds that will help supply secure housing, nutritional meals, basic physical health care, substance abuse care and aftercare, mental health counseling, personal development and empowerment, job assessment, training, and placement assistance. The system we have created to help reach these goals will allow you to sponsor a veteran directly for only $5 per month.

  • Date October 15, 2020
  • Location Joplin, Missouri

Sponsor A Veterans
campaign

There's a lot of veterans who won't want to use our main service for a variety of reasons. With our main goal being to help veterans in general, we've got our random act of kindness - Veterans Edition. Your monthly subscription helps generate funds that allows us to do everything from helping with the mortgage to buying a decent meal for the veteran and their dependents It's only $5, and it will go a long way.

"Our sponsorship and Random Act of Kindness programs give back directly to veterans in need. Your $5/mo directly goes to veterans in need or you can submit a veteran to sponsor directly."
